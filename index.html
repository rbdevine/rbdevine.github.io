C02 On the Rise

C02 output into the atmosphere due to human activity has been on the rise in the past 4 decades.

Earth's Land and Sea Temperature

The surface temperature of the earth on both land and water, relatively stable for thousands of years, began a sharp incline with the industrial revolution.

Glacier Surface Area Reduction

The earth glaciers located at the earth's two poles have been dramatically losing surface area in the past few decades. The trend is expected to accelerate in the decades to come.
